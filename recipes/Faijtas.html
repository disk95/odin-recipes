Fajitas

Need a simple, vibrant midweek meal the family will love? Put together these easy chicken fajitas and people can create their own masterpieces at the table

We can thank the cuisine of Tex-Mex for the classic fajita. Originally, the term referred to a grilled strip of meat served in a tortilla. These days you can find steak, chicken, shrimp, or even vegetarian fajitas. Minimal prep and minimal cook time means these can be on your dining room table FAST. Top them off with sour cream, pico de gallo, and guac and for an extra special treat go ahead and make your own flour tortillas! If you like a little extra spice on your fajitas go ahead and add some chili powder, cayenne, or paprika!

Fajitas ingredients

  • Oil
  • 1 pack of taco Seasoning
  • 1 pack tortillas
  • 500gr minced meat (can use less) You can do the usual which is a mix of beef and pork meat, but we use 100% beef.
  • Cheese, you decide what you want but go for milder cheese(not blue cheese). We buy a premade mix from the supermarket which is: Gouda, Emmentaler and mozzarella.
  • Onion, sliced
  • Lettuce, for example iceeberg lettuce
  • Sour Cream
  • Optinal:bell pepper
  • Optinal:guacamole (if you’d like you could use this instead of the salsa)

Preperation

  1. Some sunflower oil in a frying pan.
  2. Fry the minced meat till it´s nice and brown.
  3. Pour over the taco seasoning and add a bit of water. Mix the seasoning so all the meat has a bit on it.
  4. Leave this to simmer on low hear.
  5. When all water is gone you can eat!
  6. Just start putting together your fajita, start with putting on some cheese, put some meat on top of that. then add any toppings you like.
  7. Enjoy! :)
