Reuben Sandwich

These Reuben sandwiches are really delicious and easy to make. They are one of my family's fix-it-quick favorites. I like to serve them with big bowls of steaming vegetable soup and dill pickles on the side.

What is a Reuben Sandwich

A reuben is a grilled sandwich featuring corned beef on rye bread. The reuben is a menu staple in Jewish-style delicatessens, but it's not technically kosher because it combines meat and cheese.
